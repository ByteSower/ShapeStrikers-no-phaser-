📖 Unit Glossary

❓ How to Play

🎯 Goal

Clear 15 waves in Normal mode or survive the full 25-wave Void Campaign. Build an army of elemental units and use synergies, positioning, and upgrades to outlast each boss wave.

🛒 Shop Phase

Buy units from the shop, then click a tile in your zone (bottom 2 rows) to place them. Refresh the shop for new units. Right-click a placed unit to sell it, or use the Sell Unit button on mobile.

⚔️ Battle Phase

Units auto-battle! They move toward enemies and attack within their range. Faster units act first. Abilities trigger automatically when ready.

🔥 Elements & Synergies

Units belong to 8 elements: Fire, Ice, Lightning, Earth, Blood, Plague, plus Arcane and Void as unlockables. Fielding 2 or more of the same element activates synergy bonuses.

⬆️ Upgrades

Use gold to buy upgrades in the Stats tab: Army Expansion (+slots), Field Medic (healing), War Chest (interest), and more.

🎮 Controls

Click shop cards to buy → click tile to place. Click your unit → click empty tile to move, or click it again to deselect. Right-click to sell on desktop, or use the Sell Unit button on mobile. Escape to cancel.

Statuses stack (each reapplication adds +1 stack up to the cap). Barrier blocks all negative statuses.
