📖 Unit Glossary

❓ How to Play

🎯 Goal

Defeat 15 waves of enemies and 3 bosses to win! Build an army of elemental units and use synergies to gain the advantage.

🛒 Shop Phase

Buy units from the shop, then click a tile in your zone (bottom 2 rows) to place them. Refresh the shop for new units. Right-click a placed unit to sell it.

⚔️ Battle Phase

Units auto-battle! They move toward enemies and attack within their range. Faster units act first. Abilities trigger automatically when off cooldown.

🔥 Elements & Synergies

Each unit has an element: Fire, Ice, Lightning, Earth, Arcane. Having 2+ units of the same element activates synergy bonuses!

⬆️ Upgrades

Use gold to buy upgrades in the Stats tab: Army Expansion (+slots), Field Medic (healing), War Chest (interest), and more.

🎮 Controls

Click shop cards to buy → click tile to place. Click your unit → click empty tile to move. Right-click to sell. Click enemy units during battle to inspect.
